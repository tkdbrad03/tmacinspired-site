Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At TMac Inspired™, we value your privacy as much as we value your ambition. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information with the discretion and security you expect from a premium consultancy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for our "Slay Your Shift" sprint or other events.
  • Purchase a book or product.
  • Subscribe to the Tee Elite Circle newsletter.
  • Contact us directly via email.

This information typically includes your name, email address, billing information, and any professional details you choose to share regarding your pivot journey.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to facilitate your experience with TMac Inspired™. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Deliver the digital products, consulting services, or books you have requested.
  • Process transactions securely.
  • Send you "The Tee Elite Room" insights and updates (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website and service offerings.

3. Information Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except for trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies to enhance your browsing experience and understand site traffic. You may choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options, though this may affect the functionality of certain site features.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to request that we delete it ("the right to be forgotten"). To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@tmacinspired.com.
